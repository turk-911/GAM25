Special Honour - IIIT Allahabad

Special Honour for 2002-2004 Registered Batch

We are thrilled to announce that this year IIITA Alumni Affairs will pay special tribute to the batches of 2002-2004. These years marked a significant period of growth and achievement for IIIT Allahabad, and we are proud to honor the alumni who have not only excelled in their respective fields but have also remained steadfast in their commitment to the values and community of our institution.


The IIITA family will celebrate the extraordinary accomplishments of our graduates. This year, we are particularly excited to spotlight the contributions of alumni from the 2002-2004 batches. These individuals have made a lasting impact in areas such as technology, business, academia, and public service, and their achievements reflect the high standards of excellence that IIIT Allahabad strives to uphold.


Join us in celebrating these remarkable individuals who have gone above and beyond in their professional and personal lives, demonstrating exceptional dedication to IIIT Allahabad.
