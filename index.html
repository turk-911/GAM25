ABOUT THE EVENT

IIIT Allahabad is going to organize the Global Alumni Meet 2024 from 27th to 29th September 2024 at IIIT Allahabad.

A Nostalgia Trip...

We are delighted to announce that IIIT Allahabad will host the Annual Global Alumni Meet 2024 (GAM2024) on-campus from September 27th to 29th, 2024. We warmly invite you and your family to join us, revisit your Alma Mater, and reminisce about the cherished moments of your past. Online registration is now open.
