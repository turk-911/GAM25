GENERAL INFORMATION REGARDING REGISTRATION

The Institute is pleased to announce that IIIT Allahabad will host the Annual

"SILVER JUBILEE GLOBAL ALUMNI MEET 2024 (GAM 2024)"

The event is going to be arranged in a Physical mode at IIITA from 27th to 29th September 2024. We would like to invite you and your family to return to your Alma Mater to travel to the depths of your memories and relive the moments you had left behind.

1. Registration Fee Details (Deadline for registration 25th August 2024)

  • The Registration plan for Alumni is Rs. 1,000/- (refundable upon attending the inauguration event at IIITA scheduled for 28th September 2024)

    The Registration plan for each accompanying family member(s) is Rs. 1,000/- . For children below five years, there will be no additional fee. This is non-refundable.

2. Benefits for Registered Alumni (who will attend the meet on 28th September)

  • Welcome Dinner on 27th September 2024.
  • Breakfast, Lunch, and Gala Dinner on 28th September 2024.
  • Breakfast and Lunch on 29th September 2024
  • Accomodation at IIITA (Offered on First Come, First Serve basis)
  • Each registration will recieve an elegant Gift Hamper.
  • Recreational Activities for children.
  • Sangam visit on 29th September at 6:00 AM for interested alumni.
  • Free alumni card (waiver of card fee of Rs. 2,000/-)

3. Refund Policy for Already Registered Alumni

Refund of Rs. 1,000/- for each alumni registration once you attend the Inauguration Ceremony scheduled on 28th September 2024.

4. Registration Steps

  • Step 1: Register using the below register button and complete the payment. Save payment acknowledgment slip/message as an Image or PDF
  • Step 2: Fill out the google form as floated in the email/Whatsapp message/Facebook group sent to you (upload above acknowledgment slip). Google Form Link

5. Accommodation Facilities for Alumni (in IIITA Campus)

The accommodation in the IIITA campus will be provided on a first come, first serve order as there is limited availability of rooms. Therefore, you are requested to register as soon as possible/before the due date otherwise you will have to arrange the accommodation on your own.
